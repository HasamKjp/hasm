What is IEEE Sudan & its history ?

IEEE Sudan subsection Was founded in June 2011 by prof Sharif Babiker and group of sudanese technical professionals all worked hard to establish a Subsection from IEEE organization in sudan. And since the approval of IEEE sudan formation in 2011 it has been striving over years to form a technical community that allows students and professionals to practice research & participate in international conferences and competitions. Which enables connecting researchers to the global scientific community. IEEE Sudan forms many conferences & events annually and has effective student branches in universities dedicated for developing students skills & awareness of the technical field
